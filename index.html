What is Eklavya?

SRA took you from INCEPTION to MARIO, learning programming, embedded systems, OpenCV, and more.

Eklavya is a chance for you to put that knowledge to the test.

How it works

01

Logic-Building & Technical Challenge

Team up in pairs and crack a set of tasks drawn straight from the workshops. Score high enough and you move on.

02

Interview

Walk us through your solutions and pitch a summer project idea. Impress the panel and you're in the Eklavya '26 cohort.
